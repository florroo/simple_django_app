About Doctus

Doctus is a minimalist document management application designed for people who value simplicity, speed, and privacy.

The app allows you to create, store, edit, and organize your personal documents in one secure place, accessible anytime after logging in.

Built with the Django framework, Doctus focuses on clean design, server-side rendering, and reliable session-based authentication.

This project was created as a learning and portfolio application, showcasing practical backend development and UI fundamentals.
